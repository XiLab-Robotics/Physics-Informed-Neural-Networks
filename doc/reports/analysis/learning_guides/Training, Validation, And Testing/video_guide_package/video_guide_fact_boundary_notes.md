# Training, Validation, And Testing Video Guide Fact Boundary Notes

## Repository Scope Boundaries

- This guide explains evaluation discipline and workflow logic, not one single model family.
- Mention TE-specific splitting constraints, but do not claim that every future campaign already enforces every advanced validation protocol automatically.

## Safe Statements

- The repository distinguishes train, validation, and test roles in its educational material and campaign planning workflow.
- TE-specific leakage and coverage concerns are central to trustworthy evaluation.

## Statements To Avoid

- Avoid saying that a low validation error guarantees real deployment performance.
- Avoid saying that the test set is used throughout training.
- Avoid claiming that random splitting alone is always adequate for TE data.
