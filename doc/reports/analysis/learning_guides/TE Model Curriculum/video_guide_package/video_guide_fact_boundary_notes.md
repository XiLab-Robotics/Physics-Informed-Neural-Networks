# TE Model Curriculum Video Guide Fact Boundary Notes

## Implementation Status Guardrail

- This guide mixes currently implemented families, near-term planned families, and longer-horizon research directions.
- The narration must preserve that distinction explicitly.

## Safe Statements

- MLP / feedforward and harmonic regression are established starting points in the curriculum.
- Periodic-feature and residual-harmonic directions already have dedicated explanatory reports in the repository.
- The curriculum intentionally expands toward temporal, hybrid, and physics-informed families.

## Planned Or Roadmap-Oriented Statements

- GRU, LSTM, TCN, mixture-based, transformer, state-space, neural ODE, and PINN discussions should be framed as curriculum or roadmap material unless a separate implementation report is cited.
- The curriculum figure is a study map, not a claim that every family is already in active production use.

## Statements To Avoid

- Avoid saying that all listed families are implemented.
- Avoid saying that the most advanced family is automatically the best solution for TE.
- Avoid presenting PINNs or transformers as the natural starting point for this repository.
