# Neural Network Foundations Concept Video Source Brief

## Audience

- learners who want a neutral explanation of Neural Network Foundations
- students who need a clear chapter-by-chapter educational video source
- viewers who want model, workflow, or concept understanding before repository-specific framing

## Video Goal

Explain Neural Network Foundations as the basic ideas behind neurons, layers, activations, loss minimization, and generalization.

## Target Depth

- beginner-friendly intuition first
- technical clarity second
- practical interpretation third

1. What the foundational concepts are and why they matter.
2. How the core learning mechanics work at a neutral level.
3. How training, validation, and testing connect to those foundations.
4. What misunderstandings should be avoided.
5. How these basics prepare the viewer for later model guides.

## Required Takeaways

- Neural Network Foundations should be understood first as the basic ideas behind neurons, layers, activations, loss minimization, and generalization.
- The explanation should stay neutral and broadly reusable outside this repository.
- Training, validation, and testing responsibilities must stay explicit when relevant.
- The viewer should leave with both intuition and practical usage awareness.

## Concepts That Must Stay Brief

## Concepts That Must Not Be Omitted
