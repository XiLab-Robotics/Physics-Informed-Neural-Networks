# Neural Network Foundations Concept Video Terminology Sheet

## Preferred Terms

| Term | Definition | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `neuron` | A weighted sum plus bias followed by an activation. | Keep it mathematical, not biological. |
| `loss function` | The scalar objective optimized during training. | Tie it to learning. |
| `generalization` | Performance on unseen data rather than memorized training samples. | This distinction is essential. |
