# Harmonic Regression Concept Video Terminology Sheet

## Preferred Terms

| Term | Definition | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `harmonic term` | One sine or cosine component in a periodic basis. | Tie it to model order. |
| `basis order` | The highest harmonic index used in the model. | Explain the flexibility tradeoff. |
| `coefficient` | A learned weight multiplying one basis term. | Keep the meaning practical. |
