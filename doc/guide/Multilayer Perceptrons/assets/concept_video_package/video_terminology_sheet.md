# Multilayer Perceptrons Concept Video Terminology Sheet

## Preferred Terms

| Term | Definition | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `multilayer perceptron` | A feedforward network made of stacked dense layers and nonlinear activations. | Use MLP as the short form. |
| `hidden layer` | An intermediate learned transformation between input and output. | Keep the wording simple. |
| `capacity` | The expressive power driven by width, depth, and parameter count. | Link it to underfit versus overfit risk. |
