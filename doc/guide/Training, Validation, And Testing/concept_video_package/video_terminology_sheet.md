# Training, Validation, And Testing Concept Video Terminology Sheet

## Preferred Terms

| Term | Definition | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `training set` | The data used to fit model parameters. | Do not conflate it with all data. |
| `validation set` | The data used for development-time choices such as stopping or model selection. | Keep the role precise. |
| `test set` | A held-back split for final unbiased evaluation. | Stress the holdout nature. |
