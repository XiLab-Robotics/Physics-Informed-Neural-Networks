# Training, Validation, And Testing Project Video Terminology Sheet

## Preferred Terms

| Term | Definition | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `held-out test` | The final untouched split used for the repository's unbiased estimate. | Central repository term. |
| `curve-level leakage` | Leakage caused by related samples from the same TE curve crossing splits. | Important project-specific risk. |
| `comparison credibility` | The trustworthiness of reported differences across model families. | Keep the wording practical. |
