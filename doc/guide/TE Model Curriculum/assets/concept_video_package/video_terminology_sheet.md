# TE Model Curriculum Concept Video Terminology Sheet

## Preferred Terms

| Term | Definition | Notes |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `curriculum` | An ordered learning and comparison path across model families. | Use this as the central metaphor. |
| `baseline family` | A simpler family used as a starting comparison point. | Differentiate from advanced families. |
| `roadmap family` | A family worth studying later but not necessarily implemented now. | Keep the boundary explicit. |
